How to Use Grade Ease

Follow these simple steps to make the most of our grading system.

Step 1: Go to Grading Page

Navigate to the grading page on the website.

Step 2: Select Files to Upload

Click on the designated area to choose and upload the files you want to be evaluated.

Step 3: Evaluate

Once the files are uploaded, initiate the evaluation process. This may involve waiting for a moment as the system processes the submitted files.

Step 4: Voila! Grades are Calculated

After the evaluation is complete, your grades will be calculated and displayed on the page. You can now view and review your results.
